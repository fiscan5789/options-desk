About this scanner

This tool implements TD Sequential as defined in DeMark's standard published rules. A scan runs against the most recently closed daily bar — if you scan during market hours, today's partial bar is excluded.

Setup phase (9): bullish (buy) setup requires 9 consecutive closes < close 4 bars earlier; bearish (sell) setup requires 9 consecutive closes > close 4 bars earlier. Setup is perfected when the low of bar 8 or 9 (buy) is ≤ the lows of bars 6 and 7, or the high of bar 8 or 9 (sell) is ≥ the highs of bars 6 and 7.

Countdown phase (13): after a completed setup, count bars where close ≤ low 2 bars earlier (buy) or close ≥ high 2 bars earlier (sell). Bars need not be consecutive. The 13-vs-8 qualifier requires bar 13's low ≤ bar 8's close (buy) or bar 13's high ≥ bar 8's close (sell); if not met, an unqualified 13 prints with Qual=No.

TDST = price extreme of the prior opposing setup, used as support (buy TDST) or resistance (sell TDST). Shown alongside current close so you can gauge whether the level is intact.

Composite quality score (0–100): Each candidate is scored on signal type (40 base for qualified 13s, 25 for perfected 9s, etc.), plus modifiers: freshness (+15 if completed today), bar efficiency (+5 for clean count, –5 for choppy), TDST confluence (+10 intact, +5 within 2%), ATR-normalized move (+10 for 1.5–3.5 ATR Goldilocks zone, –8 if parabolic), and penalties for whipsaw (opposing 9 within 10 bars: –15) or 25%+ recent moves (event-driven: –10). Liquidity floor: tickers with avg daily $-volume below $5M are excluded entirely. Star ratings: 5★ = 80+, 4★ = 65–79, 3★ = 50–64, 2★ = 35–49, 1★ = below 35.

Not implemented in this version: Recycle (R), Combo, the optional 8-vs-5 countdown deferral, intersection variants, weekly-timeframe confluence, and earnings-date filtering. The 13-vs-8 qualifier is implemented since it's standard.

Polygon endpoint used: /v2/aggs/ticker/{ticker}/range/1/day/{from}/{to}?adjusted=true
