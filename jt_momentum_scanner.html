JT MOMENTUM SCANNER

Jegadeesh-Titman cross-sectional momentum · options-overlay
Methodology. Implements Jegadeesh & Titman (1993, 2001) cross-sectional momentum. Stocks are ranked by cumulative return over the formation window (J), skipping the most recent week to avoid short-term reversal / bid-ask bounce. The top decile = winners (the long-side momentum candidates). Liquidity screens from JT (2001) exclude price < $5 and the smallest-cap names. The entry date is the next trading day; DTE target is derived from the holding period K (3-month K is the strongest documented combo, momentum decays past ~12 months).
How entry date & DTE are computed
• Entry date = next US trading day after the scan (skip-week already applied in the ranking).
• DTE target = holding period K in calendar days, with a tradable band. K=3mo → ~90 DTE (band 75–105). Because momentum is strongest in the first ~3 months and decays after 12, the scanner suggests the nearest standard monthly expiration inside the band so you capture the documented holding window with one position.
• For directional long-winner trades the scanner suggests slightly-ITM to ATM calls (delta target configurable); these are equity-overlay suggestions, not a chain pull — tap a ticker to load the live chain.
